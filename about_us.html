About Us

Mission

The major mission of our project is to uplift the small retailers selling their products through various social media means like Instagram and Facebook.
These people are work hard extensively but unfortunately, despite of immense hard work they don't get much attention and importance through these platform. Thus, our website aims at providing them with a platform of their own that can uplift them and help them reach more and more public who are genuinely interested in their products.

Vision

Our website majorly aims at uplifting small retailers with less followers and background support. Our main focus was to reach out to people producing handmade and customised products, these maybe gifts, decorative items, craft items, stationeries and other homemade accessories.
# With our website we would be able to provide wide spread reach to our local retailers and help them reach out customers beyond their confined circles.
# Honour and give importance to their handmade craft and skills thereby promoting them through our website.
# Help them receive worldwide acknowledgement and appreciation for their skills and potential.
It's just a small initiative by us to help them reach out the heights they actually deserve.

Services

The prime services that we shall offer include -:
# A platform for small retailers who are willing to sell their products online, this may comprise of the complete details of the products including quality, quantity, pricing and feedback from our customers.
# We shall also provide mandatory contact details of the retailers to directly contact them regarding important enquires and consultations.
